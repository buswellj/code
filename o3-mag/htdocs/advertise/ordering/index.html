Overview
To advertise in an upcoming issue of o3 magazine, simply contact our sales team at (408) 754-7350 or via email to sales@o3magazine.com.

Deadlines
Deadlines are the first of the month prior to the issue you want to advertise in. For the July 2006 issue, the deadline is June 1st 2006. Placement in the publication is on a first come, first serve basis. If media is not received by 9pm PST on the 1st, it will be delayed an issue. Payment must be received by the 15th of the month prior to the issue. Payment not received by the 15th of the month will result in the ad being pulled, NO EXCEPTIONS!!

Payment
Company Checks or Money Orders drawn on US Funds only. Must be made payable to Spliced Networks LLC

Media
Media must be in JPEG format in US Letter (8.5" x 11") at 300 DPI. RGB or CMYK, although RGB is preferred. Use maximum quality, no compression, baseline optimization is ok. This is a 2550 x 3300 pixel file at 300 dpi. Simply email the media to sales@o3magazine.com.
