promoting o3

Overview
 
The launch of o3 back in November was a huge success. We view that success as a great starting point, and continue to market and promote o3 on a daily basis.

Media Sponsorship
The o3 marketing team has a pro-active approach to media sponsorship. In 2006, we have sponsored events such as SCALE 4x, LinuxAsia 2006, Canada on Rails YVR06, and spring (br /) 2006. Such sponsorship works both ways, our readers our informed of quality conferences, while conference attendees learn about o3 magazine.

Advertising
o3 magazine is advertised in a number of other publications, including Free Software Magazine, [IN]SECURE Magazine, and hakin9.

Events
o3 magazine operates booths at a number of events. o3 magazine is also promoted at events where Spliced Networks exhibits as well.

Distribution
o3 magazine operates a private distribution network, our marketing team is working night and day to extend this network. The distribution network works with educational institutions, professional groups, user groups, businesses and business groups to promote and deliver each issue of o3 to their members.
