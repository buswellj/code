Articles for Advertisements

Overview
 
The program has a simple concept, with a powerful premise for any small business owner or new startup looking to gain traction in the IT marketplace. Simply submit an article in Open Office 1.0 (sxw) format. Our editorial team will review the article. If we choose to include it in o3 magazine, you will receive credit for one full page advertisement. If we chose not to include it in the magazine, but it is good enough to include on our site, we'll give you credit for a free column advertisement.

Guidelines

 
  • - One Article for One Advertisement
  • - We accept English articles only at this time
  • - Please proof read and spell check your document
  • - Open Office 1.x (sxw) format ONLY!!!
  • - Attach images / figures as JPEG or PNG
  •  
  • - Send articles to submit@o3magazine.com

Advertisements
 
When your article has been accepted for inclusion in o3 magazine. You can contact our sales team who will already have your details. If you don't have an advertisement, we will typically assist small businesses free of charge to put one together. Credit can only be redeemed for an advertisement, credit must be used within 12 months.
