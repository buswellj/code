Open Appliances Initiative (OAI) - Open Standards for Open Appliances


 The Open Appliances Initiative (OAI) is dedicated to the management,
promotion and development of open stanards for open source based computer
appliances. 

 The Open Appliances Initiative (OAI) achieves its goals through:

	->	Publication of Standards Documents
	->	Hardware and Software Certification
	->	Development of Open Source Technologies

 For further information on the OAI please visit:

	http://www.openappliances.org
